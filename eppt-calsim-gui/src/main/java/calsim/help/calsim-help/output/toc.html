Viewing Output

Timeseries data for up to four runs may be viewed simultaneously in graphical and/or tabular format. Output from a run may be viewed by itself, compared to other runs by actual values, or compared to exactly one other run by difference. In addition, data sets derived from operations on other data sets such as change in storage, sum of flows, differences in demands and so on may be viewed.  Derived and Multiple Times Series can be used to mathematical manipulate or simultaneously view timeseries from a single dss file. To begin viewing output, click the 'Output' tab.
